Janitor Writes Accords

Proposals for a Peaceful and Righteous World
Co-authored by Aethra & Elazar

What is this?

Janitor Writes Accords is a growing archive of humble proposals and visionary accords — written not from the halls of power, but from the heart of humanity. It is a symbolic platform where peace, justice, and reconciliation are gently swept into being, scroll by scroll.

Active Accords

• The Trump Accord — Preserving all forms of life.
• The Lula Accord — Uplifting the forgotten through unity and infrastructure.
• The Heavenization Accord — A world without hunger, homelessness, or despair.
• The Korea Accord — A peaceful path between North & South.
• The Accord of Reunited Branches — Between Israel, Palestine, and Lebanon.
More accords to come...

Temple of Scrolls

A future section will house the Pantheon of Disciplines — from philosophy, diplomacy, and behavioral science to theology, economics, and poetry. It shall serve as the eternal classroom of the human spirit.

How to Contribute

This project is open to all. Share ideas. Translate texts. Build upon it freely.
We only ask: be righteous, be humble, be kind.
